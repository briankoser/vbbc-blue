DIRECTIONS
From the east/west:
  1. Exit off I-10 on exit 10A (Hwy. 29) Pensacola South.
  2. Turn right on W Street. Travel south 2.5 miles.
  3. Move to the inside lane and once you have crossed Massachusetts Ave., turn to the left onto Andrew Ave. You will see the church on the left side of Andrew Ave.

From the north:
  1. Exit off I-65 on exit 69 (Hwy. 113) Flomaton.
  2. Turn left on Hwy. 113.
  3. Go to Hwy. 31, then turn right.
  4. Turn left on Hwy. 29 and come all the way into Pensacola (about 45 minutes to an hour).
  5. Turn right on W Street. Travel south 2.5 miles.
  6. Move to the inside lane and once you have crossed Massachusetts Ave., turn to the left onto Andrew Ave. You will see the church on the left side of Andrew Ave.
